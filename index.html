i'm an incoming phd student at mit's brain and cognitive sciences, working with josh tenenbaum. previously i was a student and postbac at boston college, where i studied psychology, mathematics, and computer science (minor), and worked with joshua hartshorne, liane young, and stefano anzellotti. at a high level, i'm interested in probing the flexibility of human algorithms for decision making, planning, and exploration, and how people adapt their strategies and representations to the task at hand. to do so, i primarily draw on computational methods from bayesian statistics, reinforcement learning, and probabilistic machine learning.
