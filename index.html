I'm a research assistant in the Morality Lab and Social and Cognitive Computational Neuroscience Lab at Boston College, with Liane Young and Stefano Anzellotti, respectively. Previously I was a student at Boston College, where I studied Psychology, Mathematics, and Computer Science (minor), and worked in the Language Learning Lab with Josh Hartshorne. At a high level, I'm interested in how structure in tasks and representations can influence and guide human learning in high dimensional, sparse, and dynamic environments. To do so, I draw on computational methods from Bayesian Statistics, Reinforcement Learning, and Deep Neural Networks.
